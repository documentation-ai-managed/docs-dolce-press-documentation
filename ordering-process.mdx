## How to Order

Ordering custom printing services from Dolce Press is straightforward. Our online system guides you through each step, from initial design to final delivery.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up for a free account on our website to access the ordering system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Product" icon="shopping-cart">
    Choose from our catalog of products or select custom design services.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Details" icon="file-text">
    Enter your specifications including quantity, paper type, and printing method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Design" icon="upload">
    Submit your design files or request our design services.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Quote" icon="dollar-sign">
    Receive an instant quote and estimated delivery date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve Proof" icon="check-circle">
    Review and approve digital proofs before production begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Payment" icon="credit-card">
    Secure online payment processing with multiple options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Production" icon="cog">
    Your order enters production with quality control at every stage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

import requests

# Python example for order submission
def submit_order(order_details):
    url = 'https://api.dolcepress.com/orders'
    headers = {'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_TOKEN'}
    response = requests.post(url, json=order_details, headers=headers)
    return response.json()
